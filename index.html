Get Your Data

  1. Visit Google Takeout.
  2. Sign into the right account (the one with your Google Calendar).
  3. Under "Select data to include", click "Deselect all".
  4. Scroll down and check the box next to "Google Calendar".
  5. Click "Next step" at the bottom of the page.
  6. Click "Create export" at the bottom of the page.
  7. Check the email you signed in with and click "Download your files". You might have to click on the link multiple times until you see the page below:
  8. Click "Download".
  9. Navigate to your downloads folder, select the downloaded zip file (filename starts with "takeout-" followed by numbers), right-click the zip file, then click "Extract All...", and then after the pop up click "Extract".
  10. Now it's time to upload your file below. Click "Choose File", then in your downloads folder find the folder (not zip file) "takeout-XXXXX" and click into it. Click "Takeout" then click "Calendar" then double click the file with your email.
