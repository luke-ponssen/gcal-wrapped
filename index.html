How to Download Your ICS File from Google Takeout

  1. Visit Google Takeout.
  2. Under "Select data to include", click "Deselect all".
  3. Scroll down and check the box next to "Google Calendar".
  4. Click "Next step" at the bottom of the page.
  5. Choose your preferred export options and click "Create export".
  6. Once the export is ready, download the archive file and extract it.
  7. Inside the extracted folder, find the `.ics` file(s) for your calendar data.
